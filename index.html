Beyond Your Borders | Recruitment '19

Tuesday, March 12, 2019 – Wednesday, March 13, 2019

Everyone has dreams and goals. However, very few of us actually achieve them. This is because the majority of us let our limits and borders hold us back. At times, we are into the situation where we are pushed against the wall. This is no less than the fearful situation to be in. But still, there are ones, who take up the challenge and prepare themselves to break all the borders and cross all their limits to achieve the impossible in their lives. The situation, event or people do not necessarily hold any control over you, but it’s always your experience, skills, and trials that mean the most in the first place. When you consciously work with your already acquired skills and depend only on your past experience, you’ll find out even for a single time that this is not enough.

Side Talks | Recruitment '19

Imitating people just because they're successful will screw your life... People give you advice based on their experience, which is directly linked to their personalities and their own skills. You're unique, and you don’t need to suffer through all your life because of one wrong advice which is considered side talk. Don't imitate, keep being unique and choose how do you want your life to be…
